Additionally, it reviews data management practices, including databases, and introduces networking principles that facilitate communication between computers. Security concerns in both software and hardware are emphasized, along with predictions about future technological trends. Overall, the book serves as a comprehensive guide for readers seeking a deeper understanding of how computers work at both hardware and software levels.
